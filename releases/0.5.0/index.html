Feed folders and mobile navigation

April 19, 2026 · Everything shipped since v0.2.0

New in 0.5.0

Feed folders

Create folders from the sidebar, drag feeds between them, rename or delete from the context menu. Click a folder header to read all its articles at once, with per-feed title and favicon on each row.

Mobile swipe navigation

The article list and reader are side-by-side scroll-snap panels. Swipe left to read, swipe right to go back. Pure CSS, no gesture library. A floating back pill appears in the reader view.

iPhone Dynamic Island support

Safe-area insets for the notch and Dynamic Island. Content never renders under hardware cutouts in any orientation.

Also shipped since v0.2.0

Privacy & security

Tracker stripping

Tracking pixels (Facebook, Google Analytics, Feedburner) and utm_* parameters are removed from feed content before it reaches your browser.

End-to-end encrypted sync

AES-256 encryption with a four-word passphrase derived from the EFF wordlist. Your feeds sync across devices. The server cannot read them.
