The short version

Cancel anytime, keep access until the end of your current paid period. We do not pro-rate refunds for the unused portion of the current period. If something genuinely went wrong on our side, email us.

Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Open the Stripe Customer Portal (linked from your subscription confirmation email or the app's account settings).
  • Click "Cancel subscription".
  • Your access continues until the end of the current paid period. We do not bill again after cancellation.

When we issue refunds

We will refund (in full or in part, as appropriate) when:

  • You were billed in error (e.g. duplicate charge).
  • Cloud sync was unavailable for an extended outage caused by our service.
  • You purchased a subscription and never used the cloud sync feature, and you contact us within 14 days of purchase. Note: starting cloud sync constitutes use, and (for EU/EEA consumers) explicitly waives the EU 14-day withdrawal right under Art. 16(m) of Directive 2011/83/EU. See Terms § 6.

When we don't issue refunds

  • For the unused portion of a paid period after a cancellation, unless one of the cases above applies.
  • For annual subscriptions cancelled mid-year, unless one of the cases above applies. Cancel before renewal if you don't want to be billed again.
  • For dissatisfaction with features that were clearly advertised before purchase.

How to request a refund

Email support@feedzero.app with your Stripe customer ID (visible in your subscription confirmation email or in the Customer Portal) and a brief description of what happened. We respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are issued via Stripe to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days.

Statutory rights

This policy does not affect your statutory rights. For Swiss consumers, the mandatory provisions of Swiss federal law apply. For EU/EEA consumers, mandatory EU consumer-protection rules (Directive 2011/83/EU and national implementing legislation) continue to apply notwithstanding that FeedZero is established in Switzerland.
